# AboutIAM Vision & Product Roadmap

Version: 1.0

> Goal: Make AboutIAM the world's most comprehensive, vendor-neutral
> Identity & Access Management platform covering beginners through
> enterprise architects.

## Vision

Build the **MDN + Roadmap.sh + OWASP + Wikipedia + Interactive Labs +
LeetCode for IAM**.

Guiding principles: - Beginner → Expert progression - Interactive before
theoretical - 100% browser-native where practical - Vendor-neutral - RFC
and standards driven - Hands-on learning

------------------------------------------------------------------------
