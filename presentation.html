🎯 Why We Built This

IAM is business-critical but famously dry, fragmented across hundreds of standards (OAuth, SAML, SCIM, WebAuthn), and hard to learn without a live environment.

😵‍💫

The Problem

IAM knowledge is scattered across RFCs, vendor docs, and tribal knowledge — dry theory with no safe place to practice.

💡

The Idea

One interactive academy where anyone can see a protocol in action instead of just reading about it.

🚀

The Outcome

A free, always-on training & reference platform for engineers, security teams, and new hires — no procurement needed.

🏛️ How It Works

Three principles shape every page on the platform.

01

👆 Show, Don't Tell

02

🪜 Beginner → Expert

Every topic pairs a simple analogy ("SAML = a visa-stamped passport") with the official spec.

03

🔐 Zero Backend, Full Privacy

All crypto & policy checks run in-browser. Nothing typed here ever leaves the device.
